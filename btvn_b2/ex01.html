Low and Slow

This week I am extremely excited about cooking technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to. In his book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Potter describes it as ultra-low temperature poaching.
